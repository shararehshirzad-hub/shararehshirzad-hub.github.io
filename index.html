Sustainable & Smart Infrastructure Materials

Biochar-enabled cementitious systems, self-sensing concrete, and circular construction.

I develop low-carbon, multifunctional construction materials by integrating waste-derived biochar and carbon-based conductive fillers into cementitious composites—linking materials design, performance, durability, and sensing.

Research Areas

Biochar in Cementitious Composites

Particle-size engineering, hydration effects, internal curing, strength development, and durability-related transport.

Self-Sensing Concrete

Piezoresistive response under load, percolation behavior, signal stability, and sensing-performance tradeoffs.

Hybrid Conductive Fillers

Biochar + graphene/CNT systems to reduce dosage, improve dispersion, and stabilize electrical pathways.

Circular & Low-Carbon Infrastructure

Screening LCA, embodied carbon, cost sensitivity, and circular feedstock integration for construction materials.

Data & Modeling

Regression and ML for strength prediction, mix design optimization, and linking microstructure to performance.
