Sustainable Construction Materials Lab

Research Focus

Biochar Concrete

  • Carbon sequestration & sustainability: Biochar as a partial cement replacement reduces CO₂ emissions.
  • Performance enhancement: Optimizing biochar particle size, type, and pre-treatment improves workability, strength, and durability.
  • Smart infrastructure: Self-sensing, biochar-infused concrete for SHM, wireless connectivity, and energy harvesting.

Biochar & Bio-Oil Asphalt

  • Sustainable pavements: Partial replacement of petroleum-based asphalt binders with biochar and bio-oil.
  • Durability & resilience: Enhances aging resistance, healing efficiency, and environmental performance.

Broader Impact

The lab promotes low-carbon, circular construction, transforms waste into high-value materials, and advances smart, durable infrastructure for a sustainable future.
